WHAT IT DOES
─────────────────────────────────────────────

BeautifyAds hides cluttered UI elements on Google Ads
and gives you a keyboard shortcut to jump directly to
Search terms. All settings persist across sessions.


HOW TO INSTALL (TAKES 2 MINUTES)
─────────────────────────────────────────────

1. Unzip this folder somewhere on your computer
   (e.g. ~/Downloads/beautifyads or Desktop)

2. Open Google Chrome and go to:
   chrome://extensions

3. In the top-right corner, turn ON
   "Developer mode"

4. Click the "Load unpacked" button (top-left)

5. Select the unzipped "beautifyads" folder

6. The BeautifyAds icon appears in your toolbar

7. Visit https://ads.google.com and enjoy a
   cleaner interface


WHAT'S INCLUDED IN v1.0
─────────────────────────────────────────────

MENU REORGANIZATION (the main feature)
  Keywords, Search terms, Assets, and Videos
  are grouped under the Campaigns menu where
  they belong — instead of buried in Audiences
  and Insights. Works in both the main left
  menu and the hover flyout menu.

KEYBOARD SHORTCUT
  Press G then L on any Google Ads page to jump
  directly to Search terms for the current
  campaign / ad group.

HIDDEN BY DEFAULT (toggle in popup → Clean-up tab)
  ✓ Recommendations banner on Campaigns view
  ✓ Search terms insights info box
  ✓ AI Max section in Campaign settings
  ✓ Broad match keyword section in Campaign settings

HIDDEN MENU ITEMS (re-enable in popup → Menu items)
  ✓ Brand report, Shops, Campaign groups
  ✓ Performance planner, Reach planner
  ✓ App advertising hub
  ✓ YouTube creator partnerships
  ✓ Content suitability
  ✓ Policy manager, Ad preview and diagnosis

ONE-CLICK ACTIVE TOGGLE
  Pause all customizations instantly to see
  the original Google Ads layout — without
  uninstalling. Re-enable just as easily.

STATUS BADGE
  Small badge in the top-left of Google Ads pages
  confirms BeautifyAds is active. Can be hidden in
  settings.


USAGE
─────────────────────────────────────────────

Click the BeautifyAds icon in your Chrome toolbar
to open the control panel.

  CLEAN-UP TAB
    Toggle which UI clutter blocks to hide.

  MENU ITEMS TAB
    Re-enable menu items you actually use.
    They appear in their original Google Ads
    location.

  SETTINGS TAB
    Turn the keyboard shortcut, status badge,
    or menu changes on/off.
    Reset everything to defaults.

All changes save automatically and apply instantly
on any open Google Ads tab.


FEATURE REQUESTS

Click "Request a feature" at the bottom of the

TROUBLESHOOTING
─────────────────────────────────────────────

The extension only works on https://ads.google.com
pages. If you don't see changes:

  1. Refresh the Google Ads tab
  2. Check that BeautifyAds is enabled at
     chrome://extensions
  3. Open the popup and verify your toggles

If Google updates their interface, some elements
may stop being hidden — we update the extension
to match.


PRIVACY
─────────────────────────────────────────────

BeautifyAds runs entirely on your computer.
No data is sent anywhere. All your settings
are stored locally in Chrome.

We don't track you. We don't collect anything.
